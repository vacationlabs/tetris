How do I play the game?

Everything works just like the classical Tetris you've all been used to. Use the following direction keys to play:

  • Keyboard ↓ - Move down
  • Keyboard → - Move right
  • Keyboard ← - Move left
  • Keyboard ↑ - Rotate
